HOW TO PLAY!

FORMAT:

  • each level has 7 questions in total, and you must correctly answer at least 5 questions to pass.
  • completion of a level will grant you at least 1 star; 1 star represents 5 questions correct; 2 stars represents 6 questions correct; 3 stars represents ALL questions correct.
  • in order to unlock the LEVEL 8 (the final exam), you must achieve 15 stars.

RULES:

  • each question will present a sign, and you have 4 choices to select the right answer.
  • you may choose to administer a hint if needed, which will eliminate 2 wrong answers to give you a 50-50 chance.
  • you may choose to preview a summary of the level prior to playing.
